About Me

Hi, I'm Christina - a holistically minded women's health practitioner who feels strongly that you deserve to live with an understanding of your body rather than feeling like your body is a problem preventing you from the life you desire.

I started exploring this work because I needed it. For years, I lived with a growing number of symptoms that seemed random, and when I sought out the help of my doctor, I was met with band-aid solutions and no real answers at best - dismissive or gaslighting behaviors at worst.

That was when I officially decided it was time to lean into my intuition, the quiet whispers, to follow a path entirely my own. That path led me to the cyclical wisdom that has always been there, even when it was quieted for some time.

I learned that my body isn't complicated; it is timed, wise, and constantly speaking to me. As soon as I decided to listen, my energy shifted, my emotional patterns made sense, and when I became ready to think about conception, it happened smoothly. I realized I'd already begun that journey the day I said yes to listening, supporting, and accepting.

Now, I guide women who are walking that same path. Women who want to feel better in their bodies, understand their hormones and female body, and prepare themselves for pregnancy or transition through perimenopause grounded, and ready to feel good in the next chapter of life.

My approach blends:

  • cycle awareness & symptom interpretation
  • nervous system support
  • nutrition & minerals
  • emotional & intuitive listening
  • herbal & holistic therapies
  • the gentle encouragement that every woman needs to know she's on the right track

You don't have to figure this out alone, and your body is NOT working against you - it's trying to speak to you. I'm here to help you hear it.
